Frequently Asked Questions

What are the clinic's opening hours?

Our clinic is open Monday through Friday from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. and Saturday from 10:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.

Do I need to make an appointment to take my pet?

We appreciate if you can schedule an appointment beforehand to ensure we can attend to your pet properly.

Do you accept different payment methods?

Yes, we accept cash, credit and debit cards.

What services do they offer?

We offer a wide range of veterinary services, including routine checkups, vaccinations, surgeries, dental care, laboratory testing and more.

What should I do if my pet has an emergency outside of business hours?

If your pet has an emergency outside our opening hours, we recommend that you contact our emergency service at Phone Emergency.
